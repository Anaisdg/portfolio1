Popular Music Insights

Three part visualization and analysis of popular music in the US: 1) An interactive map with top artists and genre for 15 major cities filterable by demographics 2) Time-lapse bubble chart highlighting the change in 30 emotions top billboard songs throughout history 3) Rotating carousel of word clouds for top songs throughout history

A three part analysis and visualization of musical tastes by artists, songs, genres, lyrics, demographics, race, and gender.


I. A geographical examination of the musical preferences across 15 cities -- filtered by artist genre, race, and gender -- based on Spotify streaming frequency.

II. Emotional influences and lyrical content mapped by analysis of 70 years of top-charting Billboard songs by genre and year.

III. Wordclouds for top songs filterable by genre throughout US history.
