News Sentiment Analysis

A sentiment analysis of the Twitter activity of various news outlets, and presentation of findings.



  • Built With:
  • • Python
  • • Twitter API
  • • vaderSentiment
  • • Matplotlib



Figure 1. A scatter plot of sentiments of the last 100 tweets sent out by each news organization, ranging from -1.0 to 1.0, where a score of 0 expresses a neutral sentiment, -1 the most negative sentiment possible, and +1 the most positive sentiment possible. Each plot point reflects the compound sentiment of a tweet. Each plot point is sorted by its relative timestamp.

Figure 2. A bar plot visualizing the overall sentiments of the last 100 tweets from each organization.
